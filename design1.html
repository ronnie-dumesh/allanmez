Design I - Tiger (Sight) - Fall 2017

The tiger’s sense of sight is directly related to it’s movement with relation to the elevation of it’s eyes. This project focuses on translating the characteristics of a tiger’s vision into spacial and visual elements that embed themselves into the terrain of an island and create an inhabitable environment that is directly related to movement and elevation.
